Чем мы занимаемся

Architecture

Make’s singular aim is to design the best buildings, places and spaces in the world. A central part of this is providing the complete design of buildings across a range of sectors. We develop conceptual designs through to the final details, before going on site to oversee construction through to practical completion and post-occupancy, to protect the continuity of the design concept and build quality.
View projects

 

Interior design

Inspired interior design enhances people’s experience of a space. We offer a fully integrated service that complements the overarching architectural concept, ensuring each project is finished to a high level of quality and detail. Whether they’re workplaces, residences, retail spaces or mixed use developments, we deliver interiors for new Make buildings, refurbishments and fit-outs for new buildings by other architects.
View projects

 

Urban design

Smart urban design helps shape the communities of the future. Working with client and community, we deliver masterplans that create vibrant new places and spaces, attract people, and encourage investment through popularity and increased activity. In the past decade we’ve transformed challenging areas into desirable destinations, revitalised disconnected city-centre sites and showcased the best the UK’s universities have to offer.
